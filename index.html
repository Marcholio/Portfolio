The Final Race

Verkkojulkaisemisen perusteet -kurssin lopputyönä toteutettu HTML-canvas + JavaScript -peli. Pelimekaniikka pohjautuu täysin Blizzardin Hearthstoneen. Toteutettu yhteistyössä Kasper Kylmälän ja Sofia Levosen kanssa.

A HTML-canvas + JavaScript -game developed as a final assignment for web development course. Game mechanics are based on Hearthstone by Blizzard. The game was developed in collaboration with Kasper Kylmälä and Sofia Levonen.

Biljardi

3D-biljardia kaksinpelinä. Peli itsessään on toteutettu Unity-pelimoottorilla, sisäinen logiikka C#:lla ja 3D-mallit SketchUpilla. Huom. ei toimi Chromella, suosittelen Firefoxia.

Billiards

3D-billiards for two players. Game is made with Unity game engine, logics with C# and 3D-models with SketchUp. Note: doesn't work with Chrome, I recommend trying Firefox instead.

Fridge camera with image recognition made with Raspberry Pi. In Addition there's also a Telegram bot to check groceries list.
